Here are a few memorable courses I have commpleted while at PSU

CS 441, Artifical Intelligence

CS 469 / 470 Capstone Team Lead

AI was an interesting course to complete. At first it was extremely difficult, due to my lack of working knowledge of Linear Algebra and Stats, thankfully it was manageable. I wanted to highlight this course specifically because once I got the code to work, it became immensely fascinating.

This class is pretty challenging, but playing a game against the AI you built and trained is pretty cool, at least for me. Mine is a Tic Tac Toe agent. Which itself is a knowable game so I wreck it, but it's still interesting to see it exploit mistakes, with it only being trained via rewards. It had to learn the rules on it's own over 500 training sesssions. 		For the capstone, I was lucky to actually be a team lead for a fantastic group of students. We worked on a project called WonderTix for Portland Playhouse.

I wanted to highlight this coursework as I am actually extremely proud of my team. Obviously, I am not the strongest web developer, so having a team I could trust and had more expertise than myself was truly amazing. The project is a new idea for Capstones, where there are actually multiple teams working on it at a time, overlapping 1 quarter with each team. So we joined another team, then they finished up their capstone, to be replaced by a new team who has taken over now that Spring is almost over.


Here is the major Product I have worked on!

Shattered is a long term project that myself and some friends started working on over 10 years ago. It grew out of the desire to play a Role Playing Game which wasn't as unbalanced as we wanted.
